Major Attractions
🗼
Perry's Victory Monument
history
352-ft Doric column commemorating the Battle of Lake Erie (War of 1812). Elevator to the observation deck — best views on the island. Walkable from downtown.
Time45–60 minCost$10 elevatorCloses~6 PM
🍷
Heineman's Winery & Crystal Cave
winery
Oldest family-owned winery in Ohio (1888). Tour includes the Crystal Cave — the world's largest geode, discovered when they dug a well. Wine tasting after.
Time45 minCost~$10–15Last tour~4–5 PM
🕳️
Perry's Cave Family Fun Center
cave
208-ft cave at 52°F year-round, guided tour. Same complex has mini golf, gemstone mining, antique car museum, and the Butterfly House.
Time25 min cave / 1–2 hr complexCost~$10 cave
🦋
Butterfly House
nature
Walk-through enclosure with ~50 butterfly species. At the Perry's Cave complex. Quiet, photogenic, low-effort.
Time20 minCost~$8
🌿
Lake Erie Islands Nature & Wildlife Center
nature
Small museum with local wildlife exhibits, raptors, and island ecology. Free / donation. North end of island — requires a longer walk or the bus.
Time30 minCostdonation
🍇
Put-in-Bay Winery
winery
Smaller and less famous than Heineman's, at the Doller Estate. Tastings available, quieter vibe.
Time30 minCosttasting fee
